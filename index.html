Research Interests

My research interests lie at the intersection of Operations Research, Machine Learning, and Decision Science — with a focus on formulating real-world business and operational challenges as tractable optimization problems. I am particularly interested in:

  • Resource Optimization & Scheduling: Workforce capacity planning, staffing allocation under uncertainty, and multi-objective scheduling formulations (LP/MIP)
  • Queueing-Theoretic Modeling: Analytical and simulation-based approaches to service system design and capacity dimensioning
  • Explainable ML for Decision Systems: Interpretable predictive models (SHAP/LIME) for high-stakes domains such as financial services, lending, and customer retention
  • AI-Driven Process Optimization: Integrating ML predictions with prescriptive optimization to close the loop from insight to action
